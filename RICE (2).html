*Rice*

Information about rice

"Rice is a grass that is a staple food for many people around the world. It is a member of the grass family and is believed to have originated in Asia."

1.Cultivation.
Rice is grown in tropical and warm temperate climates. It requires warm, moist conditions to grow. It is grown in over 100 countries on every continent except Antarctica. Rice is processed into different forms, such as brown, milled, parboiled, and enriched.
2.Nutrition.
Rice is a rich source of carbohydrates, which are the body's main fuel source. Brown rice is a good source of fiber, manganese, selenium, magnesium, and B vitamins. Rice also contains vitamins, calcium, iron, and minerals.
3.Uses
.. Rice is used as a foodstuff. It is also an ingredient in medicines and cosmetics. It has a role in crafts and religious ceremonies.
3.History
... Rice is one of the most important cultivated plants, feeding more people than any other crop. Its domestication started 8 to 13 thousand years ago. Rice is also known as 'dhanya,' which means the sustainer of the human race.

Masale Bhat


Ingredients

▢2 tbsp oil
▢1 bay leaf
▢1 inch cinnamon
▢2 pods cardamom
▢5 cloves
▢1 tsp mustard
▢1 tsp cumin
▢pinch hing
▢few curry leaves
▢2 tbsp cashew, chopped
▢½ onion, chopped
▢2 chilli, slit
▢1 inch ginger, grated
▢½ tsp turmeric
▢1 tsp chilli powder
▢1 tsp garam masala
▢1 tbsp goda masala
▢10 tindora, sliced
▢½ potato, cubed
▢3 tbsp peas
▢1 brinjal, sliced
▢1 cup rice, rinsed
▢1 tsp salt
▢½ tsp jaggery
▢3 cup water
▢2 tbsp coconut, grated
▢2 tbsp coriander, chopped

Procedure:-

1)Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil. add 1 bay leaf, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 pods cardamom, 5 cloves, 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp cumin, pinch hing and a few curry leaves. splutter the tempering. Add 2 tbsp cashew and fry until the cashew turns crunchy.
2)Further add ½ onion, 2 chilli and 1 inch ginger. saute until the onion softens slightly. Keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tbsp goda masala. Saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
3)Now add 10 tindora, ½ potato, 3 tbsp peas, 1 brinjal and fry for 2 minutes or until the vegetables shrink slightly. Add 1 cup of rice. i have used sona masuri rice, soaked for 20 minutes. traditionally, it is made using Maharashtrian short-grain Ambemohar rice. However, you can also make using any short-grain rice like sonamasuri, surti kolam, kalijeera. Add 1 tsp salt, ½ tsp jaggery and saute for 2 minutes without breaking the rice. Further, add 3 cup water and mix well.
4)Add in 2 tbsp ghee, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
Once the rice is cooked well, mix gently making sure everything is well combined. 5)Finally, Masale Bhat Recipe can be served topped with coconut and coriander.
