*Wheat*

Information about Wheat

"Wheat is a widely cultivated cereal grain of the genus Triticum, and a staple food source globally. It is a grass that is grown for its seeds, which are used to make flour for various food products like bread, pasta, and more. Wheat cultivation dates back to around 9600 BCE in the Fertile Crescent. In India, it is a significant rabi (winter) season crop, playing a vital role in stabilizing food grain production. ."

1.Cultivation.
Wheat cultivation typically involves preparing the soil, planting seeds in lines, managing nutrient and water resources, and protecting the crop from pests and diseases. The timing of planting, seed rate, and row spacing are crucial for optimal growth and yield, especially in India where wheat is a major rabi or winter season crop.
2.Nutrition.
Wheat is a nutritious staple food rich in carbohydrates, moderate amounts of protein, and various vitamins and minerals. Whole wheat flour, in particular, offers a good source of dietary fiber, B vitamins, and some trace minera
3.Uses
.. Wheat is primarily used for food production, especially as flour for making bread, pasta, and other baked goods. It's also a key ingredient in breakfast cereals and is used in brewing alcoholic beverages. Additionally, wheat is used for animal feed and as a source of fiber and other nutrien.
3.History
... Wheat has been cultivated for over 10,000 years, originating in the Fertile Crescent region of the Middle East. Early cultivated wheat species included einkorn, emmer, and spelt. Over time, various species of wheat have been introduced and cultivated in different regions of the world, becoming a staple food crop in many cultures.

Ghavachi Kheer


Ingredients

1 cup Broken Wheat (Dalia/ Godumai Rava
3 tablespoon Ghee
2 tablespoon Rice , soaked in water for 30 minutes
3/4 - 1 cup Jaggery , grated
3 tablespoon Fresh coconut , grated
1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg
pinch Saffron strands
2 tablespoon Chironji , or any other nuts of choice
2 cups Milk
Slivered Almonds (Badam) , or pista or cashews, for garnish.

Procedure:-

1.To begin making Maharashtrian Gavachi Kheer Recipe (Whole Cracked Wheat Pudding), first heat ghee in a saucepan.
2.Add the chironji and any other nuts.
3.Saut√© for a minute and take out the nuts onto a plate.
4.In the same pan add daliya. Stir and roast till you get the distinct aroma.
5.Take it off the heat.
6.Cool slightly and pressure cook the daliya and rice with 2 1/2-3 cups of water till it becomes soft, about 3 whistles and 10 minutes on sim.
7.In the saucepan add daliya, coconut and jaggery and heat this mixture till the jaggery dissolves. Add a little water if required.
8.Add the saffron and nutmeg to the milk.
9.Add milk and nuts to the kheer and cook for a few minutes.
10.Serve Maharashtrian Gavachi Kheer Recipe (Whole Cracked Wheat Pudding) hot or cold garnished with slivered nuts.
